Welcome to EasyForger.

EasyForger is a DSL on top of MinecraftForge that aims to make creating Minecraft Mods easier. We are still very earlier in the development, but have high hopes for the projects. If you want to have a nice introduction to the Minecraft Modding scene and what this project is, have a look at this talk, that happened during the Scala eXchange 2014 conference: https://skillsmatter.com/skillscasts/5850-minecraft-and-scala-creating-a-dsl-to-enable-kids-to-create-minecraft-mods

If you want to get involved, the best first step is to join our mailing list at https://groups.google.com/group/easyforger and follow us on twitter at twitter.com/easyforger
